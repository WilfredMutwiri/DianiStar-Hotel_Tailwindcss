~~ About DianiStar Hotel ~~

The DianiStar Hotel located at Ukunda, Kwale county, Kenya, was started in 2001 by Wilfred Mutwiri. It has served as a tourist rest and accomodation centre since it's developmet. This great hotel is ideal for ensuring customer satisfaction and pleasure. It offers a wide range of meals, ranging from recognized African dishes such as matoke to the spacious Swahili dishes such as biriani rice, coconut fish flavour, vitumbua and many more. Visit this hotel if you want to have a taste of African and Swahili dishes.
